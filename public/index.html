Services

As digital marketing evolves, so do the tools and platforms used to track these digital touchpoints. It is my aim to guide you through this ever changing world, making sure that you have the data that you need to make accurate decisions. This includes, but is not limited to the below services:

Tag Manager Setups

Tag Manager audits, migrations, setups and consultation on Tag Manager best-practices.

Web Analytics

Web Analytics setups, customisation, ecommerce tracking and analysis of results.

Data Visualisation

Data Visualisation strategy, KPI definition and building dashboards across a number of tools.

Conversion Optimisation

Setting up data-driven experiments to increase the results that most matter to you.

ABOUT ME

I am a freelance digital analytics consultant with over 8 years of experience across multiple industries.

I started off my career as a digital analyst in digital marketing agencies across Cape Town, South Africa. Having completed my Bachelor of Business Science with honours in Finance, I have a solid grounding in business and the analysis of data.
I combine this with a passion for technology and the world of digital.

After 8 years agency side, I am now focusing on offering my skills and experience on a freelance basis. I am active within the UpWork community and consult to a number of businesses across the globe.
