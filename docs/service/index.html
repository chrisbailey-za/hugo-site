Services

Digital Analytics Consulting

I specialise in technical implementations of web analytics platforms such as Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics and Matomo. Most of which is done via tag management platforms such as Google Tag Manager or Adobe DTM.

I have also spent the past five years working extensively with JavaScript due to its integral role in web analytics and tag management, which I believe sets me apart from many other digital analysts.

In addition to this, I have more than five years of data visualisation experience, designing and implementing reports and dashboards that focus on visualising actionable data, that is related to business objectives.

Tag Manager Setups

Tag Manager audits, migrations, setups and consultation on Tag Manager best-practices.

Web Analytics

Web Analytics setups, customisation, ecommerce tracking and analysis of results.

Data Visualisation

Data Visualisation strategy, KPI definition and building dashboards across a number of tools.

Conversion Optimisation

Setting up data-driven experiments to increase the results that most matter to you.
